Site Name: City_weather_app Site Purpose: My web site has the purpuse of provide weather imformation about cities around the world, additionally it will provide one or two extra parametter imformation like temperature and clouds description. Scenarios: For City_weather_app • What is the current general weather in a specific city around the world? • Where can I find contact information of the website creator? Color Schema: I chose the corlor and styles to be related to the weather. Primary Color: #0078d7 (Blue) Used for: -Header and Footer background. -Section headings (h1, h2, h3). -Left border of blockquotes in the testimonials section. -Links in navigation and other elements. Accent Color: #ffcc00 (Yellow) Used for: -Hover effect on navigation links and footer links. -Text shadow on #hero h1. -Highlighting the hover effect on interactive elements. Neutral Colors: #333 (Dark Gray): Used for body text and paragraph content. #f9f9f9 (Light Gray): Used for the background of the body. #f1f1f1 (Light Neutral Gray): Used for features and blockquote backgrounds. Typography: In my project I will select a clean and versatile typography scheme using a maximum of three fonts. These fonts are chosen for readability, aesthetic appeal, and their ability to complement the overall design. 1. Primary Font: "Lato" (Sans-Serif) Use: Body text (paragraphs). Navigation links. Buttons. Footer text. Reason: "Lato" offers excellent readability and a modern, clean look, making it perfect for general text content. 2. Secondary Font: "Roboto Slab" (Serif) Use: Section Headings (h1, h2, h3). Blockquotes in the testimonials section. Reason: "Roboto Slab" provides a strong and professional appearance with its serif style, helping headings stand out. 3. Accent Font: "Pacifico" (Handwriting) Use: Special elements such as the hero section's primary heading (#hero h1). Highlighted or decorative text (e.g., call-to-action phrases). Reason: "Pacifico" adds a friendly and creative touch to specific areas, balancing the professional tone with a hint of personality.
